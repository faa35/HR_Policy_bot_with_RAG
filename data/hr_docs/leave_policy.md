# Acme Corp — Leave Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

## 1. Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to **20 days of paid annual leave** per
calendar year. Leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per completed month of
service. New joiners accrue leave from their first day of employment.

- Up to **5 unused annual leave days** may be carried over into the next year.
  Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on 31 December.
- Annual leave must be requested at least **7 calendar days in advance** through
  the HR portal and approved by your line manager.

## 2. Sick Leave
Employees receive **10 days of paid sick leave** per year. A medical certificate
is required for any sick leave of **3 or more consecutive days**. Sick leave does
not carry over and is not paid out on termination.

## 3. Parental Leave
- **Maternity leave:** 16 weeks of fully paid leave.
- **Paternity / partner leave:** 4 weeks of fully paid leave, to be taken within
  6 months of the child's birth or adoption.

## 4. Bereavement Leave
Up to **5 days of paid leave** is granted for the death of an immediate family
member, and up to 2 days for extended family.

## 5. Unpaid Leave
Employees may request unpaid leave of up to 30 days per year, subject to manager
and HR approval. Unpaid leave does not accrue annual leave.

## 6. Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all 11 national public holidays. If a public holiday
falls on a weekend, the following Monday is observed as a holiday.
