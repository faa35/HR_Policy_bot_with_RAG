# Acme Corp — Work From Home (WFH) Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

## 1. Eligibility
All employees who have completed their **3-month probation period** are eligible
to work from home. Roles that require on-site presence (e.g. facilities, lab) are
excluded and will be notified by HR.

## 2. Hybrid Schedule
Acme operates a **hybrid model**: employees are expected to be in the office a
minimum of **3 days per week**, and may work remotely for up to **2 days per
week**. Teams may designate fixed "anchor days" when everyone is in the office.

## 3. Requesting Remote Days
- Recurring WFH days should be agreed with your manager and recorded in the HR
  portal.
- Ad-hoc remote days require **24 hours' notice** to your manager, except in
  emergencies.

## 4. Full Remote Work
Fully remote arrangements are approved only in exceptional cases and require
sign-off from both your department head and HR. Requests are reviewed every
6 months.

## 5. Equipment and Expenses
The company provides a **laptop**. Employees working from home may claim a
**one-time home-office allowance of $200** for a chair, monitor, or desk
accessories. Internet and electricity costs are not reimbursed.

## 6. Expectations While Remote
- Be reachable during **core hours of 10:00–16:00**.
- Maintain a secure internet connection and use the company VPN.
- Attend all scheduled meetings with camera on where practical.
