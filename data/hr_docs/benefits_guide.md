# Acme Corp — Benefits & Bonus Guide

_Last updated: January 2026_

## 1. Health Insurance
All full-time employees and their dependents are covered under the company
health plan from **day one**. The company pays 100% of the employee premium and
70% of dependent premiums. Dental and vision are included.

## 2. Retirement / Pension
The company matches employee retirement contributions **up to 5% of base salary**.
Matching begins after 6 months of service and vests fully after 2 years.

## 3. Performance Bonus
Eligible employees may receive an **annual performance bonus of up to 15% of base
salary**, based on individual and company performance. Bonuses are paid in March
for the previous calendar year. You must be employed on the payout date to
receive the bonus.

## 4. Referral Bonus
Employees who refer a candidate that is hired and completes **3 months** of
employment receive a **$1,000 referral bonus**. To apply:
1. Submit the candidate through the **"Refer a Friend"** section of the HR portal
   before the candidate applies.
2. Once the referred hire passes 3 months, the bonus is added to the next payroll.
There is no limit on the number of referrals.

## 5. Learning & Development
Each employee has an annual **learning budget of $1,000** for courses,
conferences, or certifications relevant to their role. Approval from your manager
is required before booking.

## 6. Wellness
- Free access to an Employee Assistance Program (EAP) with confidential counselling.
- **$50 monthly gym/wellness reimbursement** upon submitting a receipt.
