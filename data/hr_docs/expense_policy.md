# Acme Corp — Expense & Reimbursement Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

## 1. General Rules
Employees may be reimbursed for **reasonable, business-related expenses**. All
claims must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense via the HR portal,
with a valid receipt attached. Claims without receipts will not be reimbursed.

## 2. Travel
- **Flights:** Economy class for flights under 6 hours; business class may be
  approved by a department head for flights over 6 hours.
- **Mileage:** Personal car use is reimbursed at **$0.50 per mile**.
- **Accommodation:** Up to **$180 per night** for hotels. Anything above requires
  pre-approval.

## 3. Meals (Per Diem)
When travelling for business, employees receive a **per diem of $60 per day** to
cover meals. Alcohol is not reimbursable.

## 4. Client Entertainment
Client entertainment expenses must be pre-approved by your manager and must list
the names and companies of all attendees on the claim.

## 5. Office Supplies & Software
Purchases under **$100** can be claimed directly. Purchases of $100 or more
require manager approval **before** the purchase is made.

## 6. Reimbursement Timeline
Approved expenses are paid into your bank account with the **next monthly payroll
run**, provided the claim was approved at least 5 working days before payroll.
